What is UnSupp0rted?

By placing this script on your site, you will be able to notify all Internet Explorer users their browser is out of date and your web site will function much better by using a more modern browser such as Firefox or Chrome.

UnSupp0rted Installation Instructions

Follow these quick and easy steps and all people that browse your site with Internet Explorer will see a nice banner directing them to use a more modern browser.

Step 1: Copy the code snippet below

<script src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-latest.js">

Step 2: Paste the snippet anywhere on the page.

By convention, place in the <head> with your other javascript files

Step 3: Make sure the snippet gets placed on every page and clear your cache.

That's it! Go find your Uncle's computer and fire up Internet Explorer to see the banner.
Compare it to our demo that shows the banner regardless of your browser. (Link will open in a new tab)
